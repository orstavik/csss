Background

$bgColor $linear $radial

$bgColor — solid color

Background color only. Combine with $bg() for gradients or images.

$bgColor(#6366f1)
$bgColor(#8b5cf6)
$bgColor(#ec4899)

$linear — linear gradient

Angle or side, color stops, optional position/size.

$bg(linear(135deg, #6366f1, #8b5cf6))

$radial — radial gradient

Shape, position, color stops.

$bg(circle(#f0f0f0, #ccc))
