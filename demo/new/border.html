Border

$border $Border + radius

$border — width, style, color

Shorthand: width, style, color. $Border adds default radius.

$Border(1px, solid, #ddd)
$Border(2px, dashed, #6366f1)
$Border(3px, dotted, #ec4899)

$Border + radius()

Logical radius with radius(…) or shorthand r().

$Border(…, radius(8px))
$Border(…, r(24px))
$Border(…, radius(8px,24px,8px,24px))
